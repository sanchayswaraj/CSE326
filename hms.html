BENEFITS OF STAYING IN HOSTEL

Amenities & Facilities

24x7 Library Access

LPU offers a huge Library System with 11 branches. There are more than 43 lakh books and e-books on all subject fields. Library system subscribes number of journals, magazines and newspapers.

Utilize Your Precious Time

The residential faculty & staff provide after-hours guidance and support to the students, in case they need any academic or life advice. Students have opportunity to become independent! Students residing in the campus can earn while studying by taking up part time jobs in labs, library, sales, shopping mall etc.

Safe & Secure

Manned by over 400 full time security officials, thousands of security cameras, LPU is one of the safest campuses in the world. The University has its very own fire station department. LPU also has Turnstile gates at major entry/exit points as well as boom barriers for vehicles ensuring that every movement is monitored.

Access of Indoor Sports Stadium

With facilities like swimming pool, warm up pool, shooting range, squash court, badminton court, a volleyball court, basketball court etc. at 50% subsidized fee.
